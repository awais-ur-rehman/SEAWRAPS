About SeaWraps

We're on a mission to revolutionize packaging with sustainable, ocean-friendly solutions.

Our Story

SeaWraps began in 2018 when our founder, a marine biologist, witnessed firsthand the devastating impact of plastic pollution on ocean ecosystems.

After years of research and development, we created a revolutionary packaging material made entirely from sustainably harvested seaweed that completely breaks down in natural environments within weeks, not centuries.

What started as a small experiment in a coastal laboratory has grown into a global movement to eliminate plastic waste one package at a time. Today, SeaWraps is at the forefront of the sustainable packaging revolution.

Our Mission

To make sustainable food packaging solutions accessible to everyone and eliminate plastic waste in our oceans and landfills.

Our Vision

A world where packaging enhances rather than harms our environment, where marine ecosystems thrive, and where waste is a concept of the past.

Our Values

Sustainability

Every decision we make considers its long-term impact on our planet. We strive for net-positive operations in everything we do.

Innovation

We're constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in sustainable materials, never settling for the status quo.

Community

We believe in uplifting the communities we work with, from seaweed farmers to manufacturing partners and end consumers.
