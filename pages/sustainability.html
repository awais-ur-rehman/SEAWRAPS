Our Sustainability Commitment

We're dedicated to creating packaging solutions that protect our oceans and environment for future generations.

Our Oceans Need Help

Every year, over 8 million tons of plastic enter our oceans, decimating marine ecosystems and threatening over 700 species with extinction.

See Our Solutions

The Problem

Plastic Never Goes Away

Conventional plastic takes 400+ years to break down, fragmenting into microplastics that contaminate our water, soil, and food supply.

Marine Life Devastation

Over 100,000 marine mammals and one million seabirds die each year from ingesting or becoming entangled in plastic waste.

Human Health Impact

Microplastics have been found in human blood, lungs, and placentas, with unknown long-term health consequences for future generations.

Our Solution

SeaWraps has developed a revolutionary packaging material made entirely from sustainably harvested seaweed that completely breaks down in natural environments within weeks, not centuries.

Our products are not only 100% biodegradable but also actively nourish marine ecosystems if they end up in the ocean, turning the problem into a solution.

By using SeaWraps, you're not just avoiding harm - you're actively contributing to healthier oceans and a more sustainable future.

Sustainable Seaweed Farming

Regenerative Ocean Farming

Our seaweed is grown using regenerative ocean farming techniques that actually improve marine environments:

  • Seaweed absorbs carbon dioxide and nitrogen, reducing ocean acidification
  • Provides habitat for marine life, increasing biodiversity
  • Requires no fertilizers, pesticides, or freshwater to grow
  • Creates sustainable livelihoods for coastal communities

Every SeaWraps product helps expand our seaweed farming operations, creating a virtuous cycle of environmental restoration.
